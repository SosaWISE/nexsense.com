Compensation

Inside Sales Representative receives a guaranteed hourly rate of $10/hour. Average target compensation: $20-30/hour.

Flexible schedules available

Department

Call Center/Inside Sales

Summary of Functions

Providing "wow" to our Customers on a daily basis by providing education and awareness on Nexsense Products.

Culture Initiatives

Champions the "wow" customer experience by education and awareness surrounding products.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Responsible to build relationships with customers over the phone.
  • Listen attentively to customer needs and concerns; recommend solutions.
  • Educate customers on product offerings.
  • Maintain extended family repository and sales tracking systems.
  • Create a fun and family environment by participating in sales incentives, encouraging friendly competition, and fostering a united team spirit.
  • Work hard. Play hard.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Organizational Relationships

Reports directly to the Inside Sales Manager

Qualifications

  • Previous experience selling over the phone in a call center setting is preferred.
  • Previous demonstrated experience with business to consumer sales.
  • Solid communication and interpersonal skills
  • Computer proficiency in Windows environment.
  • Experience with tracking business in CRM systems, preferably salesforce.

Physical Demands

Ability to communicate orally with customers. Regular use of the telephone and e-mail for communication is essential. Sitting for extended periods is common. Hearing, vision and speaking within normal ranges is essential for normal conversations, to receive ordinary information and to prepare or inspect documents. No heavy lifting is expected. Exertion of up to 10 lbs. of force occasionally may be required. Good manual dexterity for the use of common office equipment such as computer terminals, calculator, copiers, and FAX machines.

Work Environment

The job is performed indoors in a traditional office and call center setting. Occasional and spontaneous nerf gun wars are common. Activities include extended periods of sitting with an occasional practical joke. Must thrive in a work-hard play-hard culture.
