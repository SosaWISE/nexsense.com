Culture Initiatives

  • Champions the "wow" customer service experience
  • Embraces Nexsense Core Values and treats customers, and team members based on the Core Values
  • Aim straight with a nerf gun and be not afraid to use it.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities of Customer Service Representatives

  • Position entails a high amount of customer interaction over the phone. Ensure that customers understand product information, and be able to provide additional information to customers as needed.
  • Listen attentively to customer needs and concerns; demonstrate empathy.
  • Build rapport with customers by greeting them in a courteous, friendly, and professional manner.
  • Participate in activities designed to improve customer satisfaction and business performance.
  • Communicate effectively with individuals/teams in the office to ensure high quality and timely expedition of customer requests.
  • Resolve customer complaints professionally and with empathy. Adhere to the "Give 'em the Pickle" standard
  • Work hard. Play hard.
  • Ensuring a positive customer experience by educating customers on the features and benefits of additional services.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Organizational Relationships

Customer Service Rep will report directly to the Call Center Manager.

Qualifications

  • A college degree is helpful but not required.
  • Must have good communication skills and the ability to deliver wow service to customers.
  • Solid communication and interpersonal skills
  • Previous call center experience is preferred but not required. We will train the right person.
  • Computer proficiency in Windows environment.
  • Strong attention to detail, dependability, and follow through

Physical Demands of Customer Service Representatives

Ability to communicate orally with customers. Regular use of the telephone and e-mail for communication is essential. Sitting for extended periods is common. Hearing, vision and speaking within normal ranges is essential for normal conversations, to receive ordinary information and to prepare or inspect documents. No heavy lifting is expected. Exertion of up to 10 lbs. of force occasionally may be required. Good manual dexterity for the use of common office equipment such as computer terminals, calculator, copiers, and FAX machines.

Work Environment

Customer Service Rep duties are performed indoors in a traditional office and call center setting. Occasional and spontaneous nerf gun wars are common. Activities include extended periods of sitting with an occasional practical joke. Must thrive in a work-hard play-hard culture.
